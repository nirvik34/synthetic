# Frequently Asked Questions

## Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Cryptocurrency payments are not currently supported.

**Q: Is my payment information stored?**
No. We do not store credit card details. All payments are processed
securely through our PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.

## Orders

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement by contacting support.
After 2 hours, the order enters processing and cannot be changed.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A tracking link is emailed to you within 24 hours of shipment.
You can also track your order in the 'My Orders' section of your account.

## Support

**Q: What are your support hours?**
Our support team is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST.
Emergency support for critical issues is available 24/7 via our hotline.
