REFUND POLICY
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
The product must be returned in its original condition and packaging.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receipt of the item.
Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded.

SHIPPING POLICY
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free for orders over $50.
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs an additional $15.
International shipping is available to 45 countries and takes 10-15 business days.
